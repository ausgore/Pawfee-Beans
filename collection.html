THE PURRFECT MATCH

At Pawfee Beans, we believe in giving abandoned and stray cats a home. All of them are adopted/re-homed strays and just like humans, each have very different personalities and preferences. We also house adotable cats! If you've met a cat you want to adopt, be sure to act quickly. Adoptions are on a first-come, first-serve basis based on who meets the cats and then contacting our staff.

You will be asked to take off your shoes and wash your hands! This is for our feline friends who live, eat, and sleep in the cafe. We want to keep this space as clean and hygienic as possible.
