About Us

We want to create the perfect environment to relax, unwind, and recharge. Where you can stay cosy, away from a hectic, fast-pased society and busy lifestyle. A place where you can interact with our cats safely. We offer freshly brewed coffee, top-notch tea and freshly baked pastries.

Our cats were all once strays, so don't be surprised if you recognise some of them. Bring a friend along to catch up on old times, surrounded by our furry felines!

COFFEE, TEA, PASTRIES

Come here to enjoy great coffee, tea and pastries. For the little ones and non-caffeine takers, soft drinks are available as well.

BRING-A-BUDDY

Whether you’re a cafe-hopper, a crazy cat lover, or both, quit staring at pictures of cats online while sipping your coffee. Swing by to enjoy the best of both worlds and yes, do bring a friend along!

OF COURSE, CATS

Fancy petting a furry friend while enjoying your coffee? Our humble abode is packed with many furry felines, you just have to look carefully for them. Pop by anytime, and you might just find a cuddly kitty climbing into your lap as you bask in the aroma of freshly brewed coffee.
